Enjoyed a night @enlighten_canberra with T and my furbaby. 

Enlighten is Canberra’s annual light show.

> Enlighten Festival 2026 lit up Canberra once again with eleven nights of spectacular Illuminations, stunning art installations, captivating performances, live music, food and after-dark experiences. ([Enlighten Festival](https://enlightencanberra.com/))

I’ve been to this event almost every year. Walking  around can be tiring. I had to carry my furbaby in the end 😄 it was well past his bedtime. I wonder if I would come if I didn’t have a guest 😜
